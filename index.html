Why institutions engage iMORSE.

The work is senior, evidence-led, and delivered directly. The tone is steady. The recommendations are specific.

iMORSE does not do branded frameworks. The output is the engagement: a paper a Chair can put to Council, or an answer a regulator will accept.

  • 01Senior delivery. Every engagement is led by Professor Flitman directly. No pyramid, no rotating team.
  • 02Dual fluency. Fifteen years as a university executive (DVC Research, Swinburne). Twenty-five years in consulting (Deloitte, PwC; Collaboration: Wells Advisory).
  • 03Quantitative spine. PhD in Operations Research. Fellow of the OR Society and the Australian Computer Society. Chair of the Analytics Institute of Australia.
  • 04Regulator-literate. Current TEQSA auditor. Deep familiarity with the Threshold Standards and the behaviour of Australian private and public providers.
  • 05International reach. Active engagements across Australia, Asia, the Middle East and Europe, including branch-campus and joint-venture work.
